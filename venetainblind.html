VENETIAN BLINDS

BESPOKE ALUMINIUM, PVC AND WOODEN VENETIAN BLINDS

Why not bring a touch of class to your home with our impressive selection of made to measure wooden venetian blinds? Venetian blinds offer the perfect window dressing solution for any interior. These blinds are characterized by slats which are suspended horizontally. The slats are tilted to accurately control the light passing through into your room. Wooden blinds are the ideal addition to compliment wood floors and furniture in either a contemporary or traditional setting. Natural wood adds feelings of warmth, whilst still retaining the practicality of a traditional venetian blind. Natural wooden blinds add warmth to a room, while painted white wooden blinds create a clean, chic look to your interior.
